GETTING STARTED
---------------
Launch the app from your Start Menu or Desktop shortcut.

On first launch you can:
- Add items manually or scan barcodes with your phone
- Set expiry dates and categories
- Configure notification timing (e.g. 3 days before expiry)

BARCODE SCANNER
---------------
The phone barcode scanner works from any browser on your local network.
Open the scanner URL shown in the app on your phone to start scanning.

OPTIONAL CLOUD SYNC
-------------------
By default everything stays local. If you want to sync across devices,
you can connect your own Supabase project in Settings > Sync.
