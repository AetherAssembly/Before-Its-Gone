Before It's Gone - Pantry & Expiry Tracker
==========================================

Version 1.1.0

This app tracks what's in your fridge, freezer, and pantry so nothing
expires without you noticing. Scan barcodes with your phone, set expiry
dates, and get notified before things go off.

WHAT THIS INSTALLER DOES
-------------------------
- Installs Before It's Gone on your computer
- Creates a Start Menu shortcut
- Optionally creates a Desktop shortcut
- All your data stays on your machine; no account required

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
-------------------
- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- ~200 MB of disk space

PRIVACY
-------
This app is offline-first. The only outbound requests it makes are:
- Open Food Facts (barcode lookups)
- TheMealDB (recipe suggestions)
- Your own Supabase project, only if you enable optional cloud sync

No data is sent to the developer. Full privacy policy:
https://github.com/AetherAssembly/Before-Its-Gone/blob/main/PRIVACY_POLICY.md
